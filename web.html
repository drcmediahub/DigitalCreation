OUR WEB DEVELOPMENT METHODOLOGY

REQUIREMENTS

The first step is to understand the website's Purpose, Target Audience, and Content. Content structure, wireframing, linking pages and features, content, functionality, layout planning, and UI and UX design are included in the planning stage

ANALYSIS

Here, a detailed review of the website's content in terms of information is carried out, ensuring protection and processing required.

DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

In Design and development, your website gets tailored to your specific requirements and preferences. Here, continuous communication and information flow between developers, designers, and the website owner are necessary.

TESTING & DEBUGGING

This is when quality assurance, unit, system and beta tests take place to report issues that may need to be resolved. This may cause a forced repeat of the coding stage for debugging. If the system passes the tests, the waterfall continues forward.

DEPLOYMENT

Once the website is built entirely, Make the website live after all functional and nonfunctional testing completed.

MAINTENANCE

As time passed corrective, adaptive and perfective maintenance is carried out indefinitely to improve, update and enhance the website.
